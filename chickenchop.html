CHICKEN CHOP

Grilled chicken chop has always been one of my ultimate comfort foods. There’s something deeply satisfying about a juicy, well-seasoned chicken chop with crispy edges, paired with rich sauce and simple sides that never fail to hit the spot. I especially love eating it back in my hometown, Ipoh, where it has become part of my routine dinners with my uncles and aunties. Most of the time, it’s our go-to meal after a long gaming session at a nearby cybercafe, when everyone is tired, hungry, and just looking for something familiar and comforting. Those nights make the chicken chop taste even better, not just because of the food itself, but because of the memories and the people I share it with.
